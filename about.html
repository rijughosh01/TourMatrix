About TourMatrix

Independent, detail-driven, and passionate about meaningful travel.

Company background

TourMatrix Travel Agency was founded in Melbourne to meet growing demand for transparent, tailored travel planning. Our consultants combine global supplier relationships with local accountability — so every itinerary is both inspiring and dependable.

We began as a two-person boutique desk and now serve leisure, honeymoon, and corporate clients across Australia, with a focus on long-haul Europe, Asia, and the Pacific.

Mission

To design journeys that respect your time and budget while exceeding expectations for comfort, authenticity, and support — before, during, and after travel.

Vision

To be India's most trusted independent agency for bespoke holidays and corporate travel, recognised for integrity, innovation, and human service in a digital age.

Customer commitment

We commit to clear pricing, proactive communication, and impartial advice. We do not push products that do not fit your brief. In an emergency abroad, you reach a real person on our after-hours line — not a chatbot.
