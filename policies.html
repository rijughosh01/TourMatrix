Policies & transparency

How deposits, cancellations, and documents work before we wire real payments (e.g. Stripe) on go-live.

Deposits & balance

A deposit confirms our planning slot and supplier holds. The balance is invoiced before ticket issue or final hotel deadline — typically 60–45 days before departure for long-haul trips.

  • Deposit percentage shown at checkout applies to package + selected add-ons in this demo.
  • Final invoice includes live airfares, taxes, and exchange variation.

Cancellations & changes

Cancellation fees follow supplier contracts (airlines, hotels, tours). We pass through only what suppliers charge us, plus an agency amendment fee where applicable. Ask your consultant for the matrix for your specific booking.

Accreditation

TourMatrix operates as an Australian travel intermediary. Replace this paragraph with your ATAS or state licence details and membership number when applicable.

Payments (Stripe)

For production, card data should never touch your server unencrypted. Use Stripe Checkout or the Payment Element with a backend endpoint that creates a PaymentIntent. A stub PHP example for XAMPP lives in api/stripe-create-intent.php.example — copy to api/stripe-create-intent.php, add Composer’s Stripe SDK, and set keys via environment variables.

Itinerary documents

After final payment we issue a PDF itinerary and e-tickets where applicable. Place a branded PDF at assets/itinerary-placeholder.pdf if you want a downloadable sample from the confirmation page.
